The Whisper of the Stars

Beneath the vast and endless sky,
Where whispers of the stars drift by,
Their twinkle tells a secret tale,
Of cosmic dreams and midnight sail.

A melody of light and hue,
In shadows deep, in skies so blue,
They hum a tune, serene, divine,
A lullaby for hearts that pine.

So gaze above, let wonders start,
Let starlight soothe your weary heart,
For in their glow, a truth abides,
The universe within us hides.
